# CloudBSS — Shop Owner's Guide

Welcome 👋 This guide shows you how to run your shop on CloudBSS — taking orders on WhatsApp, sending deliveries, getting paid, and managing your team. No technical knowledge needed. If you can use WhatsApp, you can do this.

---

## 1. Signing in

You'll get a login email and password from us (or from your shop's owner).

- Go to **your CloudBSS web address → /app/login** (we'll send you the exact link).
- Enter your email and password.
- You land in your **Panel** — your shop's control room.

Tip: add the page to your phone's home screen so it opens like an app.

---

## 2. Your panel at a glance

Down the left you'll find:

- **Dashboard** — today's orders and sales at a glance.
- **Orders** — every order: new, packed, on the way, delivered.
- **Chats** — your live WhatsApp conversations; reply by hand any time.
- **Cashbook** 💰 — record payments and money in/out, see cash on hand.
- **Staff** 👤 — add or remove logins for your team.
- **POS** — sell over the counter (Pro plan).
- **Products / Category** — your catalogue.
- **Dispatch / Riders** — send deliveries and track them (Pro plan).
- **Reports** — best sellers and busy days (Pro plan).
- **Setup** 🚀 — connect WhatsApp and set up your assistant.

What you can see depends on your plan (Free / Starter / Pro).

---

## 3. Connect your WhatsApp (one time)

Go to **Setup → Connect WhatsApp**.

1. A QR code appears.
2. On the shop phone: open WhatsApp → **Linked devices** → **Link a device** → scan the code.
3. It says **Connected**. That's it — your number now answers automatically.

You keep your own number. Customers message you exactly as before. There is nothing for them to download.

> If customer messages ever stop showing in **Chats**, open Chats and tap **🔗 Re-link**.

---

## 4. Set up your assistant

In **Setup → Set up your assistant**:

1. Type your business name, what you sell, your town, and any delivery note.
2. Tap **Generate welcome message** — it writes a friendly greeting for you.
3. Edit it if you like, then **Save assistant**.

Then add your **Products** (name, price, photo). The assistant uses these to answer customers and price their orders.

---

## 5. Taking orders (the day-to-day)

1. A customer messages your WhatsApp. The assistant greets them, answers questions, and takes the order.
2. You get a **new-order alert** on WhatsApp, and the order appears in **Orders** with its number (e.g. #FS-0271).
3. Pack it, then mark it **Packed**.
4. **Send a rider** (Pro) — the customer gets a live tracking link.
5. Mark it **Delivered** when it arrives.

You can jump into any chat under **Chats** and tap **Take over** to reply yourself — the assistant steps back until you hand it back.

---

## 6. Getting paid (Cashbook) 💰

This is how you track money and how the customer knows you received it.

**When a customer pays for an order:**

1. Go to **Cashbook → Record a payment for an order**.
2. Pick the order from the list (it shows who still owes how much).
3. Enter the amount, choose how they paid (cash, Mobile Money, card, bank).
4. Keep **"WhatsApp the customer a receipt"** ticked, then **Record payment**.

The customer instantly gets a WhatsApp:
- *"✅ Payment received: UGX 11,500 for order #FS-0271. Paid in full — thank you!"*
- or, for part payment: *"…Balance left: UGX 5,000."*

**Money out:** use **Add money in / out** to record expenses, supplier payments, or owner draws. The top of the page always shows your **cash on hand**.

---

## 7. Managing your team (Staff) 👤

Go to **Staff** to give your team their own logins.

- See how many logins you've used (e.g. *1 of 2 used*).
- **Add a staff login**: their name, email and a password (6+ characters). They sign in at **/app/login**.
- **Remove** a login any time (you can't remove yourself or the last login).

How many logins you get depends on your plan:

| Plan | Staff logins |
|------|--------------|
| Free | 1 |
| Starter | 2 |
| Pro | Unlimited |

If you've used all your logins, you'll see an **Upgrade** link — moving to a higher plan adds more.

---

## 8. Scheduled deliveries 🗓️

Some customers want their order *later* — after work, or tomorrow morning. Open **Scheduled**, pick the order, and choose a time (today later / tomorrow / a custom date & time).

CloudBSS then reminds you on WhatsApp so nothing slips:
- **2 hours before** — start preparing it.
- **30 minutes before** — pack it and assign a rider.
- **At delivery time** — dispatch now.

The queue shows everything coming up, grouped by day, with a countdown to each delivery.

## 9. Marketing to your customers 📣

Open **Marketing** to send a promotion on WhatsApp — a message, your products, an image and a "Reply BUY to order" call to action.

1. Tap an **AI idea** (Weekend special, New arrivals, Clear overstock, Slow movers) to have it draft the message for you — then edit it to sound like you.
2. Choose **who** receives it: all customers, recent ones, inactive ones, your VIPs, or buyers of a certain category. You'll see roughly how many people that is.
3. Tick the products to feature, add an image link if you have one, and **send now** or **schedule** for later.

> ⚠️ **Please send responsibly.** Only message customers who know you, and don't blast huge lists too often — WhatsApp can block a number that sends too much, too fast. CloudBSS deliberately sends slowly to protect you. For big or frequent campaigns, ask us to switch on the **official WhatsApp Business** connection first.

## 10. Plans & paying for CloudBSS

- **Free** — up to 30 orders a month, 1 login. Great for trying it out.
- **Starter** — unlimited orders, 2 logins.
- **Pro** — everything: counter sales (POS), riders & tracking, reports, multi-branch, unlimited logins, and the official WhatsApp option.

New shops start with a **30-day free trial** of all features. To pay or upgrade, go to **Billing** (Mobile Money or card), or ask us — we can activate it for you.
