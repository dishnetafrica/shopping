Your questions

No tech words. Just straight answers.

Do my customers need to download an app?

No. They just use WhatsApp, exactly like they already do. Nothing new to install.

Do I need a computer?

No. Everything works on the phone in your hand. A computer is fine too if you prefer a bigger screen.

I'm not good with technology — can I still use it?

Yes. We set everything up for you and stay close to help. If you can send a WhatsApp message, you can run your shop with CloudBSS.

Is my WhatsApp number safe?

Yes. It stays your number and your chats. You can switch the helper off any time and reply to customers yourself.

What does it cost?

You start free. As your orders grow, there are simple monthly plans. No big payment to begin.

Which businesses is it for?

Any shop that takes orders — groceries, food, pharmacy, hardware, beauty, fashion and more.
