Our story

Roadside recipes, city-kitchen care.

A dhaba is the roadside kitchen every Indian road-trip remembers — open flame, heavy pans, and food made with no shortcuts. We brought that spirit to Kampala: the same recipes, the same patience, served the way it should be.
